Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Tabserve ("we", "us") handles information in connection with our free mobile apps — Routevia, OneBag and RentFlow — and our automated publishing of short videos to our own YouTube channel. Contact: teknopattv@gmail.com.

1. Information we collect

Our apps are designed to work without requiring an account. Data you enter (e.g. trips, packing lists, rental records) is stored on your device and/or your own cloud storage and is used solely to provide the app's functionality. We do not sell personal data.

2. Our YouTube automation

We operate a backend automation that creates short videos from our own published website content and uploads them to our own YouTube channel using the YouTube Data API v3 (scope youtube.upload). This automation authenticates only our own channel owner via OAuth 2.0 and does not access, collect, or store data belonging to any other YouTube user. OAuth credentials are stored securely and are never shared.

Our use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

3. Third-party services

  • Image sources: Pexels (free commercial license) and Wikimedia Commons (only images with verified free licenses, with attribution shown in the video description).
  • Advertising: our free apps may show ads via Google AdMob, which may process device identifiers under its own privacy policy.
  • YouTube / Google: our video uploads use YouTube API Services. By using this feature you are bound by the YouTube Terms of Service, and the data is handled under the Google Privacy Policy (https://policies.google.com/privacy).

4. Data retention, deletion & security

We retain only what is necessary to operate the services and apply reasonable security measures to protect credentials and any stored data. We do not collect or store the personal data of any third-party YouTube user — our automation only accesses our own channel.

Any data we obtain from the YouTube API is used transiently and is not persisted beyond the upload operation. You may request deletion of any data we hold, or revoke our app's access at any time, by emailing teknopattv@gmail.com or via the Google security settings page https://security.google.com/settings/security/permissions. We will action verified deletion requests within 30 days.

5. Children

Our services are not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

6. Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected on this page with a new date.

7. Contact

Questions about this policy: teknopattv@gmail.com.
