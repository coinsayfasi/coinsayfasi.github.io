What to Pack for Kenya

What to pack for Kenya? Kenya is safari country — pack neutral, breathable layers and a warm layer for chilly morning game drives. Here's the complete carry-on packing list — and how to keep it under your airline's cabin limit with OneBag.

👕 Clothing for Kenya

  • Neutral safari colors (avoid bright/dark)
  • Lightweight long sleeves & trousers
  • Warm fleece (cold mornings)
  • Sturdy closed shoes
  • Sun hat
  • Light rain jacket

🌦️ What to wear by season

  • Dry seasons best for safari
  • Cool mornings/evenings year-round
  • Rainy seasons: waterproof

🔌 Tech & power

  • Plug: Type G, 240V
  • Universal adapter
  • Power bank
  • Phone + charger
  • eSIM / local SIM
  • Headphones

🎒 Kenya-specific essentials

  • Strong insect repellent (malaria area)
  • High-SPF sunscreen
  • Binoculars
  • Refillable water bottle
  • Headlamp
  • Power bank

📄 Documents & money

  • Passport (+ visa if required)
  • Offline copies of bookings
  • 2 payment cards (different networks)
  • Some local cash for arrival
  • Travel insurance details
  • Emergency contacts list

🧴 Toiletries (under 100ml / 3.4oz)

  • Toothbrush + mini toothpaste
  • Deodorant (solid travels best)
  • Mini shampoo / body wash
  • Sunscreen + lip balm
  • Any daily medication
  • Quick-dry travel towel
💡 OneBag tip: Small safari planes have strict (often soft-bag) limits — pack one light bag and track it in OneBag.
