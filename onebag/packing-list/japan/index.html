What to Pack for Japan

A complete carry-on packing list for Japan — covering every season, the tech you'll actually use, and the Japan-specific items most travelers forget. Keep it all under your airline's cabin limit with OneBag.

👕 Clothing

Japan is walkable — comfort beats style. Layers work year-round.

  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • Easy slip-on shoes (for temples/homes)
  • Breathable tops × 4–5
  • Light layers / cardigan
  • Packable rain jacket
  • One smart outfit
  • Socks without holes (shoes come off often!)

🍂 By season

  • Spring/Autumn: light layers, scarf
  • Summer: linen, hand fan, hat (very humid)
  • Winter: thermals, warm coat, gloves
  • Any season: compact umbrella

🔌 Tech

  • Type A power adapter (100V)
  • Power bank
  • Pocket Wi-Fi / eSIM
  • Phone + charger
  • Headphones

🇯🇵 Japan-specific essentials

  • Coin purse (lots of coins!)
  • IC card (Suica/Pasmo)
  • Small towel (many restrooms lack one)
  • Cash (some places are cash-only)
  • Reusable bag
  • Hand sanitizer
