What to Pack for Vietnam

What to pack for Vietnam? Vietnam is long, hot and humid in the south, cooler in the north — pack light layers and good walking shoes. Here's the complete carry-on packing list — and how to keep it under your airline's cabin limit with OneBag.

👕 Clothing for Vietnam

  • Lightweight breathable tops
  • Quick-dry trousers & shorts
  • Walking shoes + sandals
  • Light rain jacket
  • Modest cover-up (temples)
  • A warm layer (northern winter)

🌦️ What to wear by season

  • South (warm year-round): light, breathable
  • North winter (Dec–Feb): a warm layer
  • Wet season: quick-dry, rain jacket

🔌 Tech & power

  • Plug: Type A/C, 220V
  • Universal adapter
  • Power bank
  • Phone + charger
  • eSIM / local SIM
  • Headphones

🎒 Vietnam-specific essentials

  • Insect repellent
  • Strong sunscreen
  • Reusable water bottle
  • Stomach meds
  • Quick-dry towel
  • Power bank

📄 Documents & money

  • Passport (+ visa if required)
  • Offline copies of bookings
  • 2 payment cards (different networks)
  • Some local cash for arrival
  • Travel insurance details
  • Emergency contacts list

🧴 Toiletries (under 100ml / 3.4oz)

  • Toothbrush + mini toothpaste
  • Deodorant (solid travels best)
  • Mini shampoo / body wash
  • Sunscreen + lip balm
  • Any daily medication
  • Quick-dry travel towel
💡 OneBag tip: Domestic flights (VietJet, Bamboo) are strict on cabin bags — one carry-on tracked in OneBag keeps it stress-free.
