What to Pack for Italy

A complete carry-on packing list for Italy — stylish but comfortable for cobblestone streets, with the right clothing for churches and the Italy-specific items travelers forget. Keep it under your airline's cabin limit with OneBag.

Get OneBag — App StoreGoogle Play

👗 Clothing

Italians dress well — go smart-casual and skip the athletic wear for cities.

  • Comfortable stylish shoes
  • Smart-casual tops × 4–5
  • Versatile trousers / dress
  • Light jacket or blazer
  • Scarf (covers shoulders for churches)
  • One nicer evening outfit
  • Sunglasses

🛐 Church dress code

  • Shoulders covered
  • Knees covered
  • Lightweight shawl/scarf
  • No beachwear inside

🔌 Tech

  • Type C/F/L adapter (230V)
  • Power bank
  • eSIM / local SIM
  • Phone + charger
  • Headphones

🇮🇹 Italy-specific essentials

  • Refillable water bottle (free fountains!)
  • Crossbody anti-theft bag
  • Sunscreen
  • Some cash (small shops)
  • Comfortable day bag
  • Blister plasters
