What to Pack for New Zealand

What to pack for New Zealand? New Zealand packs four seasons in a day — layers and a solid waterproof are non-negotiable. Here's the complete carry-on packing list — and how to keep it under your airline's cabin limit with OneBag.

Get OneBag — App StoreGoogle Play

👕 Clothing for New Zealand

  • Waterproof jacket
  • Warm fleece / mid-layer
  • Thermal base layer
  • Sturdy walking/hiking shoes
  • Layerable tops
  • Warm hat & gloves

🌦️ What to wear by season

  • Seasons reversed; weather changes fast
  • Summer (Dec–Feb): layers + rain shell
  • Winter (Jun–Aug): warm layers, south cold

🔌 Tech & power

  • Plug: Type I, 230V
  • Universal adapter
  • Power bank
  • Phone + charger
  • eSIM / local SIM
  • Headphones

🎒 New Zealand-specific essentials

  • High-SPF sunscreen (strong UV)
  • Refillable water bottle
  • Insect repellent (sandflies)
  • Day backpack
  • Power bank
  • Quick-dry towel

📄 Documents & money

  • Passport (+ visa if required)
  • Offline copies of bookings
  • 2 payment cards (different networks)
  • Some local cash for arrival
  • Travel insurance details
  • Emergency contacts list

🧴 Toiletries (under 100ml / 3.4oz)

  • Toothbrush + mini toothpaste
  • Deodorant (solid travels best)
  • Mini shampoo / body wash
  • Sunscreen + lip balm
  • Any daily medication
  • Quick-dry travel towel
