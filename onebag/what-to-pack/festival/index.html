What to Pack for a Music Festival

Festivals are about comfort, weather-proofing and travelling light through crowds.

Get OneBag — App StoreGoogle Play

Outfit

  • Comfortable layers
  • Sturdy boots/trainers
  • Rain poncho
  • Bucket hat & sunglasses
  • Bum bag / fanny pack

Camp (if applicable)

  • Compact tent
  • Sleeping bag
  • Roll mat
  • Headlamp

Essentials

  • Power bank (big)
  • Refillable bottle
  • Earplugs
  • SPF & wet wipes
  • Cash + ID
💡 A big power bank is essential — charging points are rare and queues are long.
💡 Wear-in your boots; festival fields and standing all day destroy new shoes.

Frequently asked questions

What should I bring to a festival?

Weather-proof layers, sturdy footwear, a large power bank, sun protection, earplugs and a secure bum bag for essentials.

How do I keep my phone charged at a festival?

Bring a high-capacity power bank fully charged — on-site charging is scarce and expensive.
