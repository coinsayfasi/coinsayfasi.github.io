What to Pack for a Summer Vacation

Hot-weather trips pack themselves — breathable fabrics, sun protection and minimal layers.

Get OneBag — App StoreGoogle Play

Clothing

  • Light tops & shorts
  • 1–2 dresses
  • Swimwear
  • Sandals & trainers
  • Light evening layer

Sun care

  • SPF 50 & after-sun
  • Sunglasses & hat
  • Lip balm with SPF

Essentials

  • Refillable bottle
  • Insect repellent
  • Power bank
  • Compact daypack
💡 Linen and cotton breathe best in heat — synthetics can feel sticky.
💡 Pack one light layer for over-air-conditioned restaurants and evening breezes.

Frequently asked questions

What to pack for a summer holiday carry-on?

Breathable light clothing, swimwear, sandals, strong sun protection and one evening layer fit comfortably in a carry-on.

How do I stay cool while travelling in summer?

Choose loose natural fabrics, a wide-brim hat and a refillable water bottle, and avoid dark heavy colours.
