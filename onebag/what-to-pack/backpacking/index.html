What to Pack for a Backpacking Trip

Long-term backpacking rewards ruthless minimalism — every gram you carry, you carry for weeks.

Get OneBag — App StoreGoogle Play

Core wardrobe

  • 3 t-shirts
  • 2 bottoms (1 zip-off)
  • Merino layers
  • Quick-dry underwear (4)
  • Packable rain jacket

Gear

  • 35–45L backpack
  • Packable daypack
  • Microfibre towel
  • Travel padlock
  • Dry sacks

Admin

  • Passport & copies
  • Travel insurance
  • Universal adapter
  • Basic meds kit
  • Money belt
💡 Lay everything out, then remove a third — you'll still have too much.
💡 Choose merino and quick-dry so you can wash in a sink and re-wear, cutting clothing count.

Frequently asked questions

What size backpack for backpacking?

A 35–45L pack forces smart choices and stays carry-on legal on most airlines — exactly what OneBag is built around.

How few clothes can I really pack?

Three tops, two bottoms and a few quick-dry layers you wash on the go is enough for weeks of travel.
