What to Pack for a Business Trip

Look sharp without checking a bag — a tight capsule of wrinkle-resistant pieces covers days of meetings.

Get OneBag — App StoreGoogle Play

Wardrobe

  • 1–2 blazers / jackets
  • 2 dress shirts/blouses
  • Trousers or skirt
  • Smart shoes
  • Versatile knit

Tech & work

  • Laptop & charger
  • Notebook & pen
  • Business cards
  • Universal adapter
  • Power bank

Grooming

  • Travel toiletry kit
  • Lint roller
  • Wrinkle-release spray
  • Spare collar stays
💡 Roll or use packing cubes and choose wrinkle-resistant wool/synthetic blends to arrive crease-free.
💡 Stick to one colour palette so every top works with every bottom — fewer items, more outfits.

Frequently asked questions

Can I do a business trip with just a carry-on?

Absolutely. A 2-colour capsule (one blazer, two shirts, one trouser) gives 4–5 meeting outfits with minimal bulk.

How do I keep clothes from wrinkling?

Roll garments or use packing cubes, hang on arrival, and a small wrinkle-release spray handles the rest.
