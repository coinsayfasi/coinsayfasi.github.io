What to Pack for a City Break

A weekend in the city needs comfortable shoes, smart-casual layers and not much else.

Get OneBag — App StoreGoogle Play

Clothing

  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • 2–3 versatile tops
  • 1 bottom + 1 smart option
  • Light jacket
  • Crossbody bag

Daily carry

  • Power bank
  • Refillable bottle
  • Compact umbrella
  • Travel adapter

Extras

  • Reusable tote
  • Basic toiletries
  • Sunglasses
💡 Pick one pair of shoes you can walk 15,000 steps in and still wear to dinner.
💡 A crossbody bag keeps valuables secure in crowds and on transit.

Frequently asked questions

What to pack for a 3-day city break?

Comfortable shoes, two or three mix-and-match outfits, one smart option, a light jacket and a day bag — easily a personal-item-only trip.

Carry-on or personal item for a weekend?

Most weekend city breaks fit a personal item if you stick to a tight 2-colour capsule.
