What to Pack for a Beach Vacation

Sun, sand and water — beach trips are light by nature, so focus on sun protection and quick-dry fabrics.

Get OneBag — App StoreGoogle Play

Clothing

  • 2–3 swimsuits
  • Cover-up / sarong
  • Light dresses or shorts
  • Linen shirt
  • Flip-flops & sandals

Sun & water

  • Reef-safe SPF 50
  • After-sun / aloe
  • Sunglasses & hat
  • Quick-dry towel
  • Dry bag for phone

Extras

  • Refillable water bottle
  • Snorkel mask (optional)
  • Power bank
  • Insect repellent
💡 Choose quick-dry fabrics so swimwear and towels are ready by morning.
💡 Pack a dry bag — it protects your phone and wallet at the beach and doubles as a laundry bag.

Frequently asked questions

What should I pack for a 7-day beach holiday carry-on?

Two or three swimsuits, a couple of light outfits, sandals, sun protection and a quick-dry towel fit easily in a carry-on.

Do I need to bring a beach towel?

A microfibre quick-dry towel packs tiny and dries fast — far better than a bulky cotton towel for travel.
