🇺🇸 Delta Baggage Allowance

Delta baggage allowance for standard economy: a carry-on up to 10 kg (56×36×23 cm), a checked bag up to 23 kg, and no free checked bag (must be purchased). Here's the full breakdown — and how to stay under the limit with OneBag.

🧳 Carry-on rules

Yes — a personal item (small bag / laptop bag) is allowed in addition. To clear the gate sizer easily, keep your cabin bag within 56×36×23 cm and under 10 kg. A soft, slightly flexible bag is more forgiving than a rigid case.

🎒 Checked baggage

The standard checked allowance is 23 kg per bag. No free checked bag (must be purchased). Overweight bags trigger fees that often cost more than the ticket — weigh before you leave home.

Delta baggage FAQ

What is Delta's carry-on weight limit?

Delta allows a cabin bag of up to 10 kg in standard economy, with maximum dimensions around 56×36×23 cm. Always confirm on the airline's official site, as limits can change by fare and route.

How big can my cabin bag be on Delta?

The Delta cabin bag size limit is approximately 56×36×23 cm (length × width × height). Soft bags that flex slightly help you fit the sizer at the gate.

Does Delta include a free checked bag?

No free checked bag (must be purchased). The standard checked bag weight limit is 23 kg. Check your specific fare — basic/light fares often exclude checked baggage.

How do I avoid Delta excess baggage fees?
