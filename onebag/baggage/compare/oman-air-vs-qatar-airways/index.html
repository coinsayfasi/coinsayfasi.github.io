Oman Air vs Qatar Airways FAQ

Which has a bigger carry-on allowance, Oman Air or Qatar Airways?

Oman Air allows up to 7 kg (55×40×20 cm) and Qatar Airways allows up to 7 kg (50×37×25 cm) in standard economy. both allow the same 7 kg cabin bag, and both include 1 free checked bag.

Does Oman Air or Qatar Airways include a free checked bag?

Oman Air: 1 free checked bag. Qatar Airways: 1 free checked bag. Always check your specific fare.

What are the checked bag weight limits for Oman Air and Qatar Airways?

Oman Air allows 25 kg per checked bag and Qatar Airways allows 30 kg. Overweight fees are charged per kg over the limit.
