Lufthansa vs British Airways FAQ

Which has a bigger carry-on allowance, Lufthansa or British Airways?

Lufthansa allows up to 8 kg (55×40×23 cm) and British Airways allows up to 23 kg (56×45×25 cm) in standard economy. British Airways allows a heavier cabin bag (23 kg vs 8 kg), and both include 1 free checked bag.

Does Lufthansa or British Airways include a free checked bag?

Lufthansa: 1 free checked bag. British Airways: 1 free checked bag. Always check your specific fare.

What are the checked bag weight limits for Lufthansa and British Airways?

Lufthansa allows 23 kg per checked bag and British Airways allows 23 kg. Overweight fees are charged per kg over the limit.
