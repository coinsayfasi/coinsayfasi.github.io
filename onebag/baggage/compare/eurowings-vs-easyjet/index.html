Eurowings vs easyJet FAQ

Which has a bigger carry-on allowance, Eurowings or easyJet?

Eurowings allows up to 8 kg (55×40×23 cm) and easyJet allows up to 15 kg (56×45×25 cm) in standard economy. easyJet allows a heavier cabin bag (15 kg vs 8 kg), and neither includes a free checked bag in the basic fare.

Does Eurowings or easyJet include a free checked bag?

Eurowings: no free checked bag. easyJet: no free checked bag. Always check your specific fare.

What are the checked bag weight limits for Eurowings and easyJet?

Eurowings allows 23 kg per checked bag and easyJet allows 23 kg. Overweight fees are charged per kg over the limit.
