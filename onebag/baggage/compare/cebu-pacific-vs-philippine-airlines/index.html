Cebu Pacific vs Philippine Airlines FAQ

Which has a bigger carry-on allowance, Cebu Pacific or Philippine Airlines?

Cebu Pacific allows up to 7 kg (56×36×23 cm) and Philippine Airlines allows up to 7 kg (56×36×23 cm) in standard economy. both allow the same 7 kg cabin bag, and Philippine Airlines includes more free checked baggage.
