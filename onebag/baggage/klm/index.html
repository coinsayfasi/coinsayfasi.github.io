🇳🇱 KLM Baggage Allowance

KLM baggage allowance for standard economy: a carry-on up to 12 kg (55×35×25 cm), a checked bag up to 23 kg, and 1 free checked bag. Here's the full breakdown — and how to stay under the limit with OneBag.

🧳 Carry-on rules

Yes — a personal item (small bag / laptop bag) is allowed in addition. To clear the gate sizer easily, keep your cabin bag within 55×35×25 cm and under 12 kg. A soft, slightly flexible bag is more forgiving than a rigid case.

🎒 Checked baggage

The standard checked allowance is 23 kg per bag. 1 free checked bag. Overweight bags trigger fees that often cost more than the ticket — weigh before you leave home.

KLM baggage FAQ

What is KLM's carry-on weight limit?

KLM allows a cabin bag of up to 12 kg in standard economy, with maximum dimensions around 55×35×25 cm. Always confirm on the airline's official site, as limits can change by fare and route.

How big can my cabin bag be on KLM?

The KLM cabin bag size limit is approximately 55×35×25 cm (length × width × height). Soft bags that flex slightly help you fit the sizer at the gate.

Does KLM include a free checked bag?

1 free checked bag. The standard checked bag weight limit is 23 kg. Check your specific fare — basic/light fares often exclude checked baggage.
