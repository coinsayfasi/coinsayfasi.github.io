Frequently asked questions

Is OneBag free?

Yes. OneBag is free to download and use. A one-time-feel Pro upgrade unlocks extras like PDF export and packing insights.

Does it work without internet?

Absolutely. OneBag is offline-first — all your trips and lists are stored on your device and work with no connection.

How does carry-on weight tracking work?

Add weights to your items and OneBag shows a live bar against your airline's cabin limit, so you know if you're over before you reach the airport.

Which languages are supported?

OneBag is available in 6 languages, with localized item suggestions for destinations around the world.

Which platforms is it on?

OneBag is available on both iOS (App Store) and Android (Google Play).
